Southern Hemisphere Institute of Technology is a premiere establishment providing world-class higher education right here in New Zealand. They take pride in what they do to the community at large. The institute's web portal uses a service-oriented architecture with a UI front-end, and a service back-end. The previous assignments asked you to help constructing the service back-end, and this assignment is the start of your journey helping to construct the front-end. This assignment looks at the separation of data from the UI, and how such data can be consumed and used in the end-user application. A later assignment will explore potential vulnerabilities that could arise when such an architecture is poorly implemented. In the previous assignments, you implemented a small number of data sources that supply key information content from/to the institute. These data sources enable separation of information content from the presentation.
